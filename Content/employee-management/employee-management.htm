Employee management

As store manager, set up new employees and manage their access in Employee management. This is where you

  • Add new employees and set their initial log in and password.

  • Edit employee information like name address, whether they are hourly or salary, and password expiration date.

  • Grant access to specific menus and submenus.

  • Terminate employees.

Each employee needs an Employee number. These are user-specific meaning each user has a different Employee number. Employee numbers track each employee's actions. Because Employee numbers are associated with the user's action history, you cannot delete them from .

Multi store ownership

Employee numbers are store-specific which means the same Employee number can exist at different stores. To avoid conflicts between multiple stores, assign users the same Employee number at all stores. This ensures tracking each employee and also makes it easy for employees to remember their log in.
