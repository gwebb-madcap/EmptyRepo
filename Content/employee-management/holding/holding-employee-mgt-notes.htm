If using Phone Room, add the same employee in all stores. For example, Store 001 (central store), Store 002, and Store 003 are configured for Phone room. If Employee 24 is using Phone room at Store 001 to complete orders, then Employee 24 must be active at Store 002 and Store 003 to print delivery orders at those stores.
