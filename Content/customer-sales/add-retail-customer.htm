Add a retail customer account

  • Process
  • Guides
  • Videos
  • Tab 4

Retail customer accounts are typically created when you want to keep basic company information on file (like name, address, and phone number, sales history) for customers who do not have a charge account. When a customer has a Retail customer account, their company business information is included on invoices which may be useful for tax purposes.

Have counter personnel create a Retail customer account when they cannot find a customer in your system. With this information on file, you can let the customer know about specials and offer special pricing.

  1. From the Invoice screen, go to Other Functions > Add retail customer.

  1. Enter the customer Name, Address, and Phone number.

  2. Select OK to create the customer account. The Invoice screen shows the customer information.

  1. Create a customer invoice to begin selling parts.
