Customer invoices

The Invoice screen (sometimes called point of sale or invoicing) is where you create and manage invoices for your customers whether they have a Regular (commercial) or Retail (individual) account. From the Invoice screen you

  • Create new invoices.

  • Open saved invoices when you are ready to tender payment and checkout the customer.

  • Check for messages about online orders, communications, or ??

  • Review and make changes to past transactions that were tendered and completed.

  • Use when you need to find parts.

  • Complete the buyout purchase order (or pickup) process.

How to get started

Start by checking messages to learn how to invoice online orders by acknowledging messages. When you're ready to invoice customers, read about creating customer invoices for any type of account.
