Test your TAMS II knowledge

Want to test what you know about ? We have included checkpoints and test throughout the help and learning center to help you test your knowledge.

The knowledge checks are only a few questions pertaining to a specific function where the tests cover a broader range of content about multiple functions that are related.

While we provide a pass/fail percentage, don't worry. We're not keeping track. The knowledge checks and tests are provided to help you identify areas you may want to learn more about.
