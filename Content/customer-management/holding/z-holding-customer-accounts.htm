Holding customer accounts

Create a customer account when you need to

  • Create an invoice for a new customer when you don't want to use the Default customer account.

  • Record customer's purchase (or sales) history.

  • Keep payment or charge account on file.

  • Associate a custom pricing, taxes or fees with a specific customer.

Original intro has mixed info about retail and commercial accounts Customer accounts store information about retail customers like contact information, preferred payment or charge account, customer pricing, taxes or fees, and sales history. Customer accounts are assigned with a 9-digit account number automatically. The account number is required to create an invoice.

Update a customer account

Does this require a permission?

You can make changes to customer information, like a new in email, delivery address, phone number, or authorized buyer.

  1. From the menu, go to Accounts receivable Alt+A >Customer information C.

  1. Enter the Customer number, name, or Search Ctrl+Alt+S for the customer.

  1. Select Edit to make changes.

  2. Change general account information, like Name, Address, Phone number on the Account Info tab.

  3. To automatically email the customer after tendering an invoice:

  • Go to the AR Info Ctrl+Shift+R tab.

  • Enter the email address in Email statements.

  1. To add authorized buyers to the account:

  • Go to the Account Info Ctrl+Shift+A tab.

  • Select Allow purchases by authorized buyers only.

  • On the Contacts Ctrl+Shift+C tab, select New .

  • Add each person and select Authorized buyer then Save .

  1. Select Save then Yes to confirm.
