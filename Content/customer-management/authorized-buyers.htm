Authorized buyers

Commercial or charge customers (with a Regular account in ) may want to restrict who can charge on their accounts. When this is the case, add approved contacts to the account and assign them as authorized buyers. When authorized buyers are added to the account, a contact is required to create an invoice for that customer. Adding authorized buyers is a 2-step process:

  • Add contact information for people who can charge on the account.

  • Enable Authorized buyers on the customer's account information.

Enable authorized buyer for the account

  1. On the Customer information screen, go to Account info.

  2. Select Allow purchases by authorized buyers only.

  3. Select Save .

    An Authorized buyer is required before adding parts to invoices for the customer.

Delete authorized buyer

You can remove authorized buyers from accounts at your customers' requests.
