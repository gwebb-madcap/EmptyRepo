NAPA categories

 categories represent rebate programs customers qualify for special pricing while you receive money back. Customers must meet the criteria to qualify for the program typically based on the type of services the customer offers. For example, an independently-owned garage offering auto repairs can qualify for the Garage category. On the other hand, vocational training facility can qualify for the Vo-tech school category.
