Manage store pickup gross profit percent

Required Is it set by APAR? 

Is it also required for independent stores? What happens if they don't set it up? 

When customers need to purchase items outside the supply chain, the Store pickup gross profit percent determines the customer's cost for the part. Store pickup gross profit percent sets a percent, lowest dollar amount (beginning), and highest dollar amount (ending) which is used to calculate the customer cost for all parts you sell outside the supply chain.

You can also set customer-specific pickup gross profit percent in Customer information. When the Pick-up GP % is set in Customer information, it determines the customer cost, and Store pickup gross profit percent is not used.
