COM130 Special invoice recap report

Company stores

Required to use report to verify return transactions match staged parts.

The shows return transactions completed when customers return resalable merchandise, cores, and defective parts. Use this report to validate daily customer returns.

Product owners, can you confirm the following list from legacy help is valid or is Jaren Schreck the only person who can confirm?

  • Voided invoices are valid. All transactions should be a cash/charge or credit/refund. Multiple voided invoices may indicate a theft problem.

  • Voided ROA receipt for cash/check payment were posted to the account. Multiple voided receipts may indicate a theft problem.

  • There are no invoices with no charge items (items sold with a zero resale cost).

  • Parts returned for credit the previous business day are in your store.

  • Core and defective returned parts are listed on the invoice and in the appropriate staging area in your store.

For No charge transactions containing a sold part and an offsetting return of a core or defect for the same part, display in No charge invoices. The returned part displays in Cores returned for credit/refund orDefectives returned for credit/refund.
