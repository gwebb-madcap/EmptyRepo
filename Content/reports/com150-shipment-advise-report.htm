The lists packing slip and part number information for shipments for distribution center orders, factory direct orders, special order, classification orders and JOEI orders sent to your store during nightly communications.

What's on the report?

The report shows the following information:

Manifest summary

Shows the shipment number, manifest number, purchase order number(s) and container number(s) associated with the shipment.

receipts

Shows the receipts, other receipts, and factory order receipts with the following information for each part on a purchase order:

  • Product line abbreviation and part number

  • Quantity ordered, quantity shipped, quantity on back order

  • Unit cost, core cost of the item, unit discount of the item, net cost of the item

  • Quantity on hand of the item, store location codes for the item, packing slip number for the item

Parts summary

The Parts (Line Part) Summary is the last section that prints, and includes receipt types T,B,C,J, and blanks. It provides the following information:

Product owners please provide a name and descriptiong for each receipt type: T, B, C J

  • Product line abbreviation, part number

  • Packing slip count, number of different packing slip numbers on which each part is referenced, quantity received

  • Description of part number, store location codes for the item
