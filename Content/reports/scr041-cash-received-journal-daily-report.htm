SCR041 Cash received report

This report provides details for the totals entered when balancing the Cash received journal. You can use it as a working copy to look for entry errors when it is Out of balance.

What's in this report?

Administrative details

If this is the final copy or working copy, this section includes:

  • Transaction date Date transaction occurred.

  • Account date Number of days the store runs End of day. If you skip a calendar day and run End of day 2 times, the account day will only increment by 1 day. Account date is used to compare the day in a month of a year to the same period in another year.

Summary of transactions

Totals entered in the Cash received journal for sales invoices, miscellaneous receipts, over/short, and paid outs.

Summary of deposits

All cash, checks, and credit card transaction deposits with Deposit type of Store deposit and To office deposit.
How to use it? 

Required to print 2 copies. Once the Cash received journal is approved, the store manager must sign in the Approved by section on the finalized version.

Print the report

From the Cash received journal screen.
