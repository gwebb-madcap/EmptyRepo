Image styles

For best resolution of TAMS screen images, increase your monitor's font size to 175%.

  1. From Windows settings, go to System, and choose Display.

  2. Select the monitor where you are taking screen shots, go to Scale and layout.

  3. In the Change the size of text, apps, and other items dropdown menu, select 175%.

Use the following selectors on images

Selector

Use when image is

img.small-screen

to use on modals, messages, and screens with a width around 600 px

img.large-screen

use on full screen images, portions of a full image screen, and screens with a width around 1100 or more px
