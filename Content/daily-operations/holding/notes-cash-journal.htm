notes cash received journal

The Cash received journal is a record of all the funds your store collects (tender during checkout) during each accounting day. When you reconcile transactions at the end of each day, you balance the Cash received journal by entering the total amount of the cash and checks you collected so funds tendered match funds received.

When funds you tendered do not match funds collected, the Cash received journal is out of balance. As you enter totals for cash, checks, verify other incoming and outgoing cash, account for overages and/or shortages, you bring the Cash received journal into balance. Once in balance, you are ready finalize the Cash received journal for the day, to close your business day.
