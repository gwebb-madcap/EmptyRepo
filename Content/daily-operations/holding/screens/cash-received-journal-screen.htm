Cash received journal screen

When you reconcile transactions at the end of each accounting day, you match the funds tendered with funds received in the Cash received journal. The Cash received journal is record of all funds collected for each accounting day. It is out of balance when the funds tendered do not match the funds collected.

Use the Cash received journal screen to balance your journal for each accounting day.

You must start the End of day before balancing the Cash received journal.

From the menu, go to Sales activities > Cash received journal.
