A behind-the-scenes look at End of day

A series of processes run in the background during End of day, like processes that close the business day, update part information, and print reports. These processes run automatically and do not require any action.

This is what happens during the End of day processes.

Process

What it does

Close the business day

Updates the accounting day, month, and year to the current day in your store's profile. Updates the Sales journal, Cash received journal, and other scheduled End of day reports.

Purge cost information and audit prices

Audits the prices of all reportable parts, and generates a report for you to review and correct invoices and rebate information.

Update inventory

Updates inventory information from DVD.

Archive reports

Archives all reports for printing later excluding optional reports that must be scheduled for archiving.

Transmit store data

Creates and transmits a financial data file. MB is testing to confirm what is in file

Update

Installs latest version of if available.

Back up server

Backs up your store server when all processes are complete.
