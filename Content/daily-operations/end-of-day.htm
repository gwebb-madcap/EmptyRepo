Required to run End of day everyday.

End of day is a process that closes your business day to ensure data is accurate and complete. Some of these processes include:

  • Generating and printing bin labels for updated price changes. If set up in Sys mgt inv controls

  • Balancing cash drawers to avoid a backup of unfinalized Cash received journals. 2 POs do not like verbiage unreconciled discrepancies is this better?

  • Processing the latest version when updates are available.

  • Backing up store data.

During End of day, these processes complete automatically and do not require your attention. But, you can learn more by taking a behind the scenes look at the processes.

  • Run End of day at the end of each business day.

  • Complete all tasks listed in the End of day checklist.

  • Have several USB drives, one to back up each business day.

  • After End of day starts, Balance cash received journal.

Start End of day

  1. Insert today's USB drive in your store's server.

  1. To start End of day, go to the menu > Sales activities > End of day.

    We recommend you complete the list of tasks in the Check list before you start End of day. Review the tasks that are not checked, and decide to complete or skip each. After you select to complete or skip, the task is checked.

  1. Review the tasks that have not been completed. Select the Action to complete tasks or choose to Skip.

    All tasks must have a check mark for completing or Skipping tasks before End of day is available.

  1. Once all tasks are completed or skipped, select Start EOD.

  1. Confirm you inserted today's USB drive in your store's server, and then select OK.

  2. Select the printer for End of day reports.

If you sold parts outside your Low and high cost verification %, the Cost invoices screen shows invoices with those parts. Confirm or make Invoice cost adjustments before continuing the End of day process. This is your last opportunity to adjust cost. Once End of day completes, cost information is final.

Where can I set up Cost verification %? Go to Product line maintenance screen Inventory > Maintenance > Product line. Low cost verification % and High cost verification %

  1. The Review and change transactions screen opens so you can make changes to today's invoices. This is your last opportunity to change payment method, add a purchase order number, or void an invoice. Once End of day is complete, invoices are final.

    The End of day monitor shows each process as it completes.

Skipping tasks

To ensure your accounting data is accurate, we want to make sure you understand what happens when you skip a task.

Task

What happens if I skip?

Bin labels exist

Does not print pending bin labels and labels accumulate.

Review report selection

Prints reports according to the current schedule.

Major account pricing corrections

Incorrect pricing remains on major account invoices. You will not have the opportunity to review and correct pricing on major accounts once End of day starts.

Review time by employee number

Closes pay period for that day.

JOEI orders exist

If JOEI purchase orders are added in the Preload edit JOEI screen, these orders are not sent to the distribution center. If no orders are added, there is no impact. Where do I add orders to the Preload edit JOEI screen?Communications > JOEI > Preload/edit DE special orders

NAPA order has not been created

Closes this business day without creating replenishment orders.

Orders exist that have not been finalized

Serves as a reminder that orders are waiting to be reviewed and finalized so they can be transmitted.

Branch store invoices exist

Does not post branch store invoices.

If you choose Post, you can post branch store invoices and update the accounts receivable balance.

There is no impact when skipping New catalog update available and Store transmission file exists. These tasks complete automatically during End of day.
