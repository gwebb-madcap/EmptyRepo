Check-in groups are a way to receive and post parts from several purchase orders at a time. Typically you create a check-in group to receive an overnight shipment; however, you can create a check-in group to receive and post multiple purchase orders anytime

For example, you may have a few special orders that did not make the overnight shipment cut off. As these parts arrive at your store, and are not on the previous day's , create a new check-in group to receive and post them. Help with a realistic example of when to create a check-in group that's not for overnight shipments.

The shows the previous day's purchase orders, parts, and quantities ordered. It also shows the Shipment number assigned to purchase orders in the shipment.

If Include interstore receipts with dc shipment is selected, transfer orders are included and assigned a Shipment number. If not, you can create check-groups with these purchase orders.

Create a check-in group

  1. From the menu, to Inventory > Purchase orders > Received goods > Create check-in group.

    The Create check-in group screen shows open purchase orders. Notice all purchase order numbers from overnight shipments have the same Shipment number.

    Please confirm it is showing "open" purchase orders only.

  1. To create a check-in group with:

  • All purchase orders in an overnight shipment and using Include interstore receipts with dc shipments, select one purchase order from the . All purchase orders assigned to the same Shipment number are automatically selected.

  • Individual purchase orders, use Shift + click to select each.

  1. To assign purchase orders to the group, select Down arrow. The selected purchase orders move to Assigned to a check-in group.

  • To remove a purchase order from the group, select it and use Up arrow. It moves back to Available.

 Modify a check-in group

  1. From the menu, go to Inventory > Purchase orders > Received goods > Select check-in group.

  1. Select a Check-in group.

  1. Select Maintain check-in group.

  • To add a purchase order, select it from Available and use Down arrow.

  • To remove a purchase order, select it from Available and use Up arrow.

    If Include interstore receipts with dc shipment is selected all purchase orders assigned to the same Shipment number return to Available.

  • Repeat as needed.

  • Select Save.
