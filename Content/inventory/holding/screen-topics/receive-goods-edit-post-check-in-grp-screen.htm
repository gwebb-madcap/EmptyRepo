Receive goods edit and post by check-in group screen

Use the Receive goods edit and post by check-in group screen to post received quantities to on-hand inventory. From this screen, you post all purchase orders assigned to a check-in group.

From the menu, go to Inventory > Purchase orders > Received goods > Edit and post.
