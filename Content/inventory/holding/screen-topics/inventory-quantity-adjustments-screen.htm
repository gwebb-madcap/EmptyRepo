Inventory quantity adjustments screen

Use the Inventory quantity adjustment screen to maintain accurate on-hand quantities.

From the menu, go to Inventory > Adjustments.

Receipts tab

Use the Receipts tab to adjust on-hand quantities for non- stock receipts and items shipped outside of distribution center, such as:

Bullet list from legacy help, is it applicable? 

  • Jobber order entry and inquiry (JOEI) orders Special orders created when the store orders from other distribution centers.

  • Merchandise transfers Orders created when a branch store receives parts from the main store; and, the main store pays the cost without recording the transaction.

Adjustments affect product line sales history information.

Items ordered in and included on an open purchase order must be posted using the Delayed stock receipts and purchase orders. Postpone until this is covered? 

If landed pricing is enabled, the cost values are displayed with landed factors applied. Is this accurate?

 Stock adjustments tab

Use the Stock adjustments tab to adjust on-hand quantities for damaged, lost or found parts, receiving mistakes, or core and defective parts.

From legacy help, is this applicable? Adjust inventory discrepancies or assortment breakdowns (selling a partial quantity or package). Any adjustment you enter updates sales history information for the product line.
