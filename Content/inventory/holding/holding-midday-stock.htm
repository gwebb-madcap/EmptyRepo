Holding midday stock

Post midday stock receipts

When parts arrive on a midday shuttle, you can post them to your on-hand inventory immediately. You do not have to wait until end of day when you process stock receipts. This is a 2-part process. First, process the stock receipt transmission to generate a purchase order. Then, post the quantity to your on-hand inventory as you normally would.

  1. Process the stock receipt transmission

  • Go to Communications > Process received transmission.

  • Select Receipts, then Process.

    If there are no stock receipts to process look for an open check-in group with those parts.

    Does the Receive goods screen display with the check-in group/PO automatically when they select Process?

  1. Post quantity to on-hand inventory.

  • Go to Inventory > Purchase orders > Received goods > Edit & post. [Alt + I] [P] [R] [E]

  • If there is only one check-in group in the shipment, the Received goods – select check-in group screen displays all purchase orders included in the shipment.

    If there are multiple check-in groups, select one to post from the check-in group list.

  • Select a purchase order to post, then select Edit & check-in group.

  • Verify the Quantity received is correct for each part. To make changes, select the part, select Edit , and enter the correct amount in Quantity received. Select Save.

  • The final prompt asks if you are ready to post the Quantity received to your on-hand inventory. Select OK to post.

  • Enter the number of copies of reports to print, and select OK.

  • Repeat for all purchase orders.
