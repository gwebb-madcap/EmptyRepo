Landing screens when you log in

The landing screens open when you log in. Your role and permissions determine if you see one or both of the landing screens. For instance, if you are a counter person and spend most of your time at the checkout counter, you'll see the Invoice screen. Assistant and store managers will see both the Invoice and menu (sometimes referred to as back office or other functions) screens. Here's what you can do in each screen:

  • TAMS menu is where you track sales, review transactions, manage inventory, generate reports, and maintain accounts receivable.

  • Invoice screen (sometimes referred to as point of sale or invoicing) is where you create customer invoices and special orders, open online catalog, get shipping estimates from , and tender payment for customer sales.

Open the menu from the Invoice screen by selecting Other functions > Other functions.

Web dashboard

Another screen you may use is the dashboard. It is a web-based screen that has some of the same functionality as the menu, like access to customer information, order maintenance, planograms, and reports.

Go to TAMS menu > Sales activities > TAMS dashboard.
