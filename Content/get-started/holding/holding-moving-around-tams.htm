The menu is where you will track sales, manage inventory, generate reports, and maintain account receivables. Below is a description of each menu, , and what it does.

The menu includes a menu and a toolbar.

Menu

What it does
Sales activities

Atl +S

 Create invoices, manage sales transactions including major account pricing, employee sales, payments on accounts, buyout or pickup orders, and the Cash received journal.
Inventory

Alt+I

 Receive, post, adjust inventory, create purchase orders, print bin labels, physical inventory counts, review minimum and maximum levels, set up and update communications, manage classifications, write offs, defective and core parts, and set up core tracking.
Communications

Alt+C

 Transmit account receivable and inventory updates, branch store invoices and orders, check part availability.

Accounts Receivable

Alt+A

Create accounts receivable terms, and pricing profiles.

System management

Alt+M

Manage system settings like company and store profile, accounting and inventory codes, data communications, maintain vendors, employee time tracking and other tasks required at end of day and end of month.
Employee management

Alt+E

 Set up employee and pay period profiles. Authorized users can review time card exceptions and pay period activity prior to closing each pay period.
Reports

Alt+R

 Define, generate, and print reports. Archive or restore reports.
Logout/in

Alt+l

 Log out of .

Help

Alt+H

Select language for screens, version, and help.
