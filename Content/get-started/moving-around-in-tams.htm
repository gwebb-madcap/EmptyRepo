Navigating screens is easy using the mouse or keyboard shortcuts. Experienced users prefer the keyboard shortcuts and navigate without even thinking about the key combinations once they have learned the shortcut keys. Read this topic to get familiar with general navigation and keyboard shortcuts for the main screens and menus.

One page print out of most common keyboard shortcuts

General navigation

In all screens, the mouse or keys move to the next or previous field.

Key

What it does

Tab

Moves to next field.

Shift+Tab

Moves to previous field.

Enter

Moves forward through most fields.

When buttons have a green outline, it means you can press Enter to select them.

Keyboard shortcuts

The toolbar is where you manage individual records, like adding a new customer account or employee. Use the mouse or the combinations.
